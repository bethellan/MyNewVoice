V87 MENU LAYOUT IMPROVEMENTS
==============================
No behaviour, schema, media storage, phrase data or speech changes.
UI/layout only.

CHANGES:

1. SETTINGS DASHBOARD — 2-column grid (560px+)
   Groups now pair up left/right: Edit | Backup at top, Clear | Exit at bottom.
   Wide sections (Test, Offline Use, Backup Size) continue to span full width.
   Result: the Settings page is ~40% shorter on phones in landscape and tablets.

2. VOICE FOLDOUT BODY — 2-col label/control layout
   The Voice section inside Settings Preferences now uses the same horizontal
   label ↔ control layout as the outer preferences rows (added in v72).
   Speech rate, pitch, popup delay, voice picker and close mode all sit in tidy
   left-label / right-control rows instead of a tall stacked column.

3. SETTINGS PANEL — max-width cap on wide screens (900px+)
   On large tablets and desktops the Settings panel now caps at 900px and
   centres within the overlay rather than stretching to full viewport width.
   Restores the modal feel without sacrificing the full-screen layout on phones.

4. TAB CARDS — slightly taller at 2-col breakpoint (580–959px)
   At 2-column layout, cards are narrower; min-height raised from 174px to
   clamp(180px, 30vw, 260px) so cards stay comfortably proportioned rather
   than feeling flat and wide.

5. SETTINGS ENTRY OVERLAY — tightened header spacing
   "Choose an option." intro text removed (buttons are self-explanatory).
   Panel top padding reduced from 22px to 18px. Minor spacing tightening.
