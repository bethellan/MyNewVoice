MyNewVoice v56 - My People relationship dropdown

Changes:
- Adds a simple Type dropdown to My People rows in the Settings content line editor.
- Dropdown options: Not set, Family, Friend, Carer, Nurse, Doctor, Neighbour, Visitor, Other.
- Stores the selected value in the existing phrase.relationship field.
- Existing non-standard relationship values are preserved and shown as the selected value until changed.
- Updating the dropdown immediately saves the My People item and refreshes the live My People submenu if it is open.

Preserved:
- Backup/export/import schema unchanged.
- Phrase/category/media IDs unchanged.
- Storage keys unchanged.
- Local images/audio unchanged.
- Speech fallback unchanged.
- Dad-facing menu and phrase row structure unchanged.
- v55 simplified Settings/About/offline status retained.
